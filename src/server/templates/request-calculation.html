On demand modelling

Our team of Oxford researchers and volunteer data scientists are happy to provide custom forecasting and modelling support for decision makers in government, international organisations, health care, and others.

By using a global epidemic model, we can analyse scenarios like demand for ICUs and hospital beds, as well as how different mitigation strategies implemented in other countries/cities can affect your region.

In future we also hope to provide estimates on effectiveness of various containment and mitigation measures, as tracked in our public database.
